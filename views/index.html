What is the people's budget?

We're are calling on the New York City Countil to defund the NYPD by at LEAST a billion dollars this year.
While City Council's statement that they plan to make these cuts is a good first step, we know our work to #DefundNYPD is just getting started.

We need to keep the pressure up to make sure that they follow through on this committment and cut at least a billion dollars from the NYPD this year. We also need to make sure that these cuts are meaningful cuts that actually reduce policing, and that the dollars are reinvested in our community's needs.

This is a People's Budget, to DEFUND the NYPD and FUND our COMMUNITIES

We're not waiting on "experts" and government officials to suggest how we should cut the NYPD and where we should spend that money ‚Äî we're asking you to suggest it.

This is an open-source budget, we want you to share your ideas on what to cut and how to invest!

How do I participate?

There are three different aspects to the People's Budget. Plug into any or all of them!

This work is open source and collaborative: we value data and justifications, but reject the idea of "expertise." The people know best what we need!
