What is the people's budget?

We're responding to the call to action to defund the NYPD by at LEAST a billion dollars this year.
While it's a good first step that the New York City Council released a joint statement saying that they plan to make these cuts, we know our work to #DefundNYPD is just getting started.

We need to keep the pressure up to make sure that they follow through on this committment and cut at least a billion dollars from the NYPD this year.
We also need to make sure that these cuts are meaningful cuts that actually reduce policing, and that the dollars are reinvested in our community needs."

So we're starting a People's Budget to DEFUND the NYPD, and FUND our COMMUNITIES.

We're not waiting on "experts" and government officials to suggest how we should cut the NYPD and where we should spend that money ‚Äî we're asking you to suggest it.
This is an open-source budget, we want you to share your ideas on what to cut and how to invest!

How do I participate?

There are three different aspects on the People's Budget: Defund the NYPD Edition. Plug in to any or all of them! Whatever interests you.
Keep in mind, this is open source, so we want data and justifications, but we reject the idea of "expertise." The people know best what we need!
