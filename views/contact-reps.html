TAKE ACTION TODAY

Putting pressure on our elected representatives works! Defunding the NYPD has become a budget possibility just in the past few days and weeks, thanks to the work of organizers and community members calling for change.

It's more important than ever that we keep the pressure on!

1. CALL YOUR REP

Call your council member today! You can look up your council member's phone number and stance on defunding the NYPD here, or check out the full tracker.

Hello, my name is ________ and I am a constituent of the Council Member. [If comfortable, name cross streets of residence.]

I am calling because I want to urge the Council Member to support the call to #DefundNYPD and only vote for a budget that includes major cuts to the NYPD, specifically cutting the NYPD budget and officer headcount by half in FY21. This is very important to me, and will affect my decision in future elections.

I also want to urge the Council Member to publicly support the call to get cops out of schools and issues that could be addressed by social services and community workers. All funds recovered from NYPD should be redirected to Black communities this upcoming fiscal year.

I hope the Council Member will consider this issue, as I believe it's important to the constituents of this district. Thank you for your time.
