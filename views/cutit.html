Your budget's way too high — you need to cut it!

Share your suggestions for programs, divisions, contracts and other parts of the NYPD we should cut! Add to others's ideas too (don't erase). The more info the better, but you don't have to fill everything out. Particularly helpful: if you have numbers share sources, and if you have info that backs up why something is bad, drop it in! Please note: moderators will remove anything hateful or inappropriate.

How do I participate?

There are three different aspects on the People's Budget: Defund the NYPD Edition. Plug in to any or all of them! Whatever interests you.
Keep in mind, this is open source, so we want data and justifications, but we reject the idea of "expertise." The people know best what we need!
